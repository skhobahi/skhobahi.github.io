About Me

  • I received my B.Sc. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), Isfahan, Iran, in 2016.

  • I am working towards my PhD degree under the supervision of Prof. Mojtaba Soltanalian at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE), University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), Chicago, IL, USA.

  • I was the recipient of the prestigious Wexler Award for 2016-2017.

Research

My research interests include Optimization Theory, Statistical Signal Processing, Machine Learning, and their applications.

  • Optimization Theory

  • Machine Learning

  • Model-Driven Deep Learning

  • Statistical Signal Processing

  • Theory of Deep Learning

  • Radar Signal Processing
